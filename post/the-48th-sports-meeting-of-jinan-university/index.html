The 48th sports meeting of Ji’nan University

2021-11-27 1 min read

Time:11.18-11.20
Place:Shipai campus of Ji’nan University
Description:There are many outstanding athletes who are skilled at various sports.They stand out from all students.Beyond that,you can also see your teachers join in it because they will form the teaching team to compete. There are not only field events but also track events in the sports meeting.It is bound to evoke intense competition.
The equipment: Place evenly hurdles,


arrange high jump bars and cushions……

The rules for taking part in the activity:You can sign up it by scaning QR code.(tips:1.Each persons is limited to sign up two items.2.Registration deadline is before October 28.)

Why the activity is so popular:If you are an athlete,you can not only release the energy of youth,but also improve the skills of sports competition.You can cheer up for the athlete who you like if you are an audience.
